Book - Modern Nature

August 2, 2025

Recently I was reunited by a book I’ve never finished but has been on my mind many times. Modern Nature by Derek Jarman. A remarkable man’s diary about his garden house and so much more. It reminds me of beauty in everything if only I’d give it a little more attention. I wholeheartedly recommend!
