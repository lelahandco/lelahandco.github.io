Grandpas and Snails

August 5, 2025

I do this things in the morning sometimes where I walk around my neighborhood park and act like I’m a curious and eager kid in a very old and slow body. I look, feel, smell, etc. It grounds me, gets me in a curious and at the same time slows me down. It’s wonderful.

Today the park gifted me a whole bunch of snails. Some making a long track over the stone path, others having climbed high in a berry bush eating the meal of a lifetime I imagine. (Un)fortunately I didn’t have my phone to take a picture, so you’ll have to trust me that they were really really cute. I even got to see insides of one of the sail when I looked close enough.
